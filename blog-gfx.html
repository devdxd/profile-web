06 a Girl

A misterious girl in the dark.

01 Yelan

She is a mysterious person who claims to work for the Ministry of Civil Affairs, but comes out as a non-entity on their list.

02 Exusiai

Exusiai is a citizen of Laterano and an employee at Penguin Logistics. Penguin Logistics has assigned her to Rhodes Island, where she assists with various operations.

03 Ai Mie

Ai is a light headed, pink haired middle-school girl who always forgets her glasses.

04 Ganyu

She is a half-qilin Adeptus under contract with the Geo Archon Morax and serves as the general secretary of the Liyue Qixing.

05 Blue Poison

Blue Poison, Sniper Operator of Rhodes Island, will attack the enemy with her venom. She is named after the blue poison dart frog (Dendrobates tinctorius "azureus").
